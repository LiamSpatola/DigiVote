Welcome.

Welcome to DigiVote, the home of digital voting.

lock

DigiVote uses an anonymous system to record votes. When you vote, your vote is recorded anonymously in DigiVote's database. At the same time, a vote record is recorded. This only includes your name and which poll or election you voted in, not who/what you voted for. This separates you and your vote, making your vote truly secret whilst still allowing you to confirm your vote was counted correctly.

how_to_vote

DigiVote makes it simple to vote. All the usual headaches and complexities are removed. Instead, DigiVote uses a simple and modern interface with clear instructions to help you cast your vote with confidence.

schedule

DigiVote allows for polls and elections to be opened and closed automatically. This means that polls and elections can be scheduled for specific time periods. This allows polls and elections to be created in advance, but only voted in once they open, and enforces closing times.

ballot

DigiVote supports multiple styles of elections. It has options for Instant Runoff Voting (IRV), Single Transferable Vote (STV), Preferential Block Voting (PBV), and Plurality Voting. This means elections can be run in many different ways to suit everyone's needs.

account_circle

DigiVote has a MyVotes feature. This allows you to easily view which polls and elections you have voted in so far. Also, DigiVote allows you to print a voting receipt, giving you peace of mind knowing that your vote was correctly counted. However, your selection is always kept secret. There is no way to know who/what you voted for.
