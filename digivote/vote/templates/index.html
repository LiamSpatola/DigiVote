DigiVote uses an anonymous system to record votes. When you vote, your vote is recorded anonymously in DigiVote's database. At the same time, a vote record is recorded. This only includes your name and which poll or election you voted in, not who/what you voted for. This separates you and your vote, making your vote truly secret whilst still allowing you to confirm your vote was counted correctly.

DigiVote makes it simple to vote. All the usual headaches and complexities are removed. Instead, DigiVote uses a simple and modern interface with clear instructions to help you cast your vote with confidence.

account_circle

DigiVote has a My Votes feature. This allows you to easily view which polls and elections you have voted in so far. Also, DigiVote allows you to print a voting receipt, giving you peace of mind knowing that your vote was correctly counted. However, your selection is always kept secret. There is no way to know who/what you voted for, and voting receipts do not show this information.
